What can be sold on Ownvibe?

On Ownvibe you can sell everything despite any prohibited items.

Why was my item deleted?

If Ownvibe believes that an item may be counterfeit or a fraud we will take it out. If this happens we will contact you via email and explain the reason why we removed it.

A buyer just bought my item, when do I get paid?

The transaction may take up to 48 hours to be processed, if after 2 days you have still not received it please contact us at hello@Ownvibe.com

When should I ship a sold item?

Please do not ship out an item unless you have confirmed that the payment is on your PayPal account. Once you have checked the payment, please proceed with the shipping as soon as possible.

How do I refund a transaction?

You can easily refund a transaction through your PayPal dashboard.
